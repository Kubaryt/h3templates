h3dm3

h3dm3 is template created by Xocenk after the succes of h3dm3.1/3.2 template in True Mirror 2 tournament.
Diffrences from 3.1/3.2 version are that there are less banned heroes and less enabled heroes. Wood and ore wagoons and symbol of knowledge aren't disabled. Max number of rounds aren't decreased to 50.

Chagnes compared to h3dm1/h3dm2.1 (they are the same):

  • Object changes: - Disabled Tavern, - Disabled Shrine of Magic Mystery (level 4), - Imp Cache value decreased from 5000 to 3000,
  • Banned heroes: Leena, Ciele, Vidomina, Thant, Solmyr and Deemer,
  • Enabled heroes: Mutare, Mutare Drake, Boragus.
