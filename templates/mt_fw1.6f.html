mt_Firewalk 1.6f

mt_Firewalk 1.6f is template created by Ignus, he is also an orginal creator. It has some minor changes compared to built-in version.
Compared to 1.6e it has one change.

    Changes compared to built-in mt_Firewalk:
  • Object changes:
    • In zone 7 (Utopias zone):
      - Max number of Dragon Utopia per zone decreased form 8 to 6,
    • In zone 1 and 2 (Player starting zone and AI zone):
      - Disabled control spells scrolls,
  • Banned Heroes: Solmyr, Aislinn, Thant, Deemer,
  • Enabled Heroes: Haart Lich, Mutare, Roland, Mutare Drake, Boragus.
  • Enabled every faction town, including players' starting faction, in zone 2 (AI zone)
  • Playing 2v2 or 1v1 with ai is impossible now.
