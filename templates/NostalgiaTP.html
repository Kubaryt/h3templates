NostalgiaTP

NostalgiaTP is template created by Ravciozo for purpose of making guild and STZ in it more equal.
Main diffrence between it and old version is that there is max 3 rolls for Town Portal and max 4 rolls for Dimension Doors in Rampart, Inferno and Necropolis, 5 in Tower and Dungeon and 6 in Conflux.

    Changes compared to Nostalgia:
  • Banned spells: Sorrow, Slayer, Fire Shield, Inferno, Counterstrike, Magic Mirror, Sacrifice
  • Banned objects in zone 3 (so called Super Treasure): warehouses, windmills, t1 and t2 lvl dwellings, t1-3 shrines,
